Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 4, 2026

RazZ Info Tech ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by RazZ Info Tech and its mobile applications including Water Reminder, NewsX, and Women Safety App.

1. Information We Collect

We only collect the information necessary to provide and improve our services. This may include:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and hardware identifiers required to ensure app functionality.
  • Usage Data & Identifiers: Anonymous analytics data regarding how you interact with our apps. Note that third-party services like AdMob and Firebase may collect "Device Identifiers" and "Approximate Location" for advertising and analytics purposes.
  • Reading Habits (NewsX): To prioritize your privacy, your bookmarks and "Recently Read" history are stored locally on your device. We do not upload or store your reading habits on our servers.
  • Location Data: (Specific to Women Safety App) we ONLY access location services during an active SOS trigger to deliver it to your trusted contacts. We do not store your location on our servers.

2. How We Use Information

The information we collect is used in the following ways:

  • To personalize your app experience and to allow us to deliver the type of content and robust features you are most interested in.
  • To improve our applications and website to better serve you.
  • To send periodic emails or notifications if you have opted in (e.g., Water Reminders).

3. Third-Party Services

Our apps may use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Services we use include:

  • Google Play Services
  • AdMob (for serving personalized and generic advertisements)
  • Firebase (for app analytics and performance monitoring)

4. Data Deletion and Retention

We believe in giving you full control over your data. Even though our apps primarily store data locally, we provide the following deletion methods:

  • Local Data: You can delete all locally stored data, including bookmarks, settings, and history, by clearing the application cache/data in your device settings or by uninstalling the app.
  • Request Deletion: For any data that may be processed by third-party services, you can contact us at the email below to request data deletion assistance.

5. News Transparency & Policy

In accordance with Google Play News Policy, NewsX provides the following transparency disclosures:

  • Source Attribution: NewsX is a news aggregator. Every article clearly displays the name and logo of the original publisher. Users are redirected to the publisher's official website to read the full content.
  • Publisher Information: NewsX provides a platform for verified news from established Indian media houses. We do not generate original news reporting.
  • Contact Information: For any content-related inquiries or publisher requests, please contact our lead developer, Kaviraj Sharma, at the email below.
